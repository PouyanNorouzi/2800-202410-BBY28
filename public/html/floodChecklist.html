FLOOD CHECKLIST

In the event of a flood, there are many things that you may need to help you survive. Below is a checklist that you can use to make sure that you are prepared and have the supplies you need. If you do not have some of these items and are in an area that is at risk for a flood, consider stocking up to be prepared.

CHECKLIST


Protect your home from flooding by placing sandbags strategically around entrances and vulnerable areas.


An emergency kit should include non-perishable food, water, first aid supplies, flashlight, batteries, and important documents.


Develop a plan with your family for evacuation routes, meeting points, and communication during a flood.


Keep a list of emergency contacts including family, friends, local authorities, and emergency services.


Protect important documents, electronics, and sentimental items by storing them in waterproof containers.


Have extra clothing and blankets to keep warm in case of evacuation or loss of power.


Ensure an adequate supply of prescription medications and basic medical supplies for any medical needs.


Know the designated evacuation routes and locations of emergency shelters in your area.


Keep cash on hand as ATMs and credit card machines may not be operational during a flood.


Review your insurance policies to ensure adequate coverage for flood damage and consider purchasing flood insurance if necessary.
