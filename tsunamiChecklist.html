TSUNAMI CHECKLIST

In the event of a tsunami, there are many things that you may need to help you survive. Below is a checklist that you can use to make sure that you are prepared and have the supplies you need. If you do not have some of these items and are in an area that is at risk for tsunamis, consider stocking up to be prepared.

CHECKLIST


This is a catch-all for any other important supplies you may need in the event of a tsunami


Since Tsunami's can knock over power lines, you may be left without power. Flashlights allow you to see if the power goes out.


As with many disasters, there is a high chance that someone will get injured. Securing a first aid kit is a must have to disinfect, bandage, and clean injuries.


Since the power may be out for long periods of time, you will need a good supply of food and water. You will want to get non-perishable food such as canned goods so you can keep them in your kit for long periods of time


The government may issue emergency reports and information in the days after the tsunami, so a portable radio will allow you to keep up with the current news


Without power, the nights may be cold. Keeping extra blankets and clothes will help you stay warm.


if anyone in your family requires medication, you should keep an emergency stash in case of an emergency. You also need to keep Tylenol and Advil in this kit


If you and your family get seperated during a tsunami, it is essential to have an emergency plan and a place to meet in case you get seperated.


Since the power will most likely be out, many places will not be able to take debit or credit cards. Having emergency cash will allow you to make purchases for whatever you need
